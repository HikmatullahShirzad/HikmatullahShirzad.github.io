Bouncing Ball Collect Game

How to Play

Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move your blue rectangle (paddle) at the bottom.
Try to catch the green circle (ball) with your paddle.
For bonus points, catch the red triangle (bonus)!
Each time you catch the ball, you get 1 point.
If you miss the ball, your score resets to 0.
Try to get as high a score as possible!

Press Space to restart the game at any time.
